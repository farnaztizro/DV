Comparative Analysis of Life Expectancy, GDP, and Population Among Top Economies

Type: Heatmap

This heatmap presents a comparative analysis of three key metrics—Life Expectancy, GDP, and Population—across five major economies: the United States, China, India, Japan, and Germany. The color intensity represents the relative magnitude of each metric, normalized for better comparison. By visualizing these metrics together, the heatmap provides an intuitive understanding of how these countries perform in terms of average life expectancy, economic output, and population size, highlighting significant patterns and differences that might not be immediately apparent from raw data alone.
