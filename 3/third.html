Trends in Total Health Expenditure Across Top 5 Countries On Total Expenditure (2000-2014)

Type: Area Chart Trend Line

This visualization presents the total health expenditure trends from the year 2000 to 2014 for top 5 countries on total Expenditure, including the United States of America, Micronesia, Sierra Leone, Sweden, and Norway. Each colored area represents the expenditure of one country, and the layers are stacked on top of each other to show the cumulative expenditure over the years.
